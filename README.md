# RoomLightVision-KonjuPowerCase

## Step into their shoes

### 1. What matters most

    Hotel ceo: Ready on time. Installing does not disturb customers. No technical issues that the customers can see.

    RoomLight investor/owner: Product sells and brings in profit.

### 2. What keeps me up at night

    Hotel ceo: Product brings more work not less. Using Roomlight takes too much time from staff.
    RoomLight investor/owner: Competitors beating us to market

### 3. Key question

    Hotel ceo: Does it  make a real difference to customer experience
    RoomLight investor/owner: How much demand does this product have? can we sell it?

### 4. What do we need from them

    Hotel ceo: Clear idea what they are looking for the RoomLight to give them
    RoomLight investor/owner: Patience to see the company take off

## Questions for stakeholders

1. For hotel Ceo: How much help do you need in designing the lighting?
2. For hotel Ceo: Does your staff need education in using the room light dashboard?
3. Would you be interested in letting us testi different experiental lighting designs in your hotel?
4. What is your main concern/fear about the product?
